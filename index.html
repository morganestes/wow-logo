WOW

Wonder of Worship

The original logo is not my idea - it was created for the Memorial Road Church of Christ Wonder of Worship (WOW) class. As soon as I saw it, I thought "I wonder if I can re-create that in CSS?" It's not a pixel-perfect re-creation, but I think it's a decent knock-off nonetheless.

This version is the one they’re using in class. The Web version is slightly different.
